<p><p>The hydraulic fracturing fluid additives market comprises various types essential for optimizing well performance. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from corrosive agents, enhancing longevity. Biocides prevent microbial growth, safeguarding fluid integrity. Crosslinkers adjust fluid viscosity for improved proppant transport and fracture conductivity. Surfactants reduce surface tension for better fluid penetration and enhanced effectiveness. Additionally, the "Others" category includes various specialized additives that serve specific functions or address unique challenges in hydraulic fracturing operations. Together, these additives play a crucial role in successful fracking processes.</p></p>
