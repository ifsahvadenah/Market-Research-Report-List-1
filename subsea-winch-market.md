<p><p>The subsea winch market includes specialized equipment like riser pull-in winches and A&R (Air and Recovery) winches. Riser pull-in winches are utilized for the installation and retrieval of subsea risers in offshore operations, ensuring efficient management of drilling and production risers. A&R winches, on the other hand, support the deployment and retrieval of subsea equipment, enhancing operational flexibility. Together, these winches play a crucial role in subsea operations, facilitating safe and efficient resource extraction in challenging environments.</p></p>
