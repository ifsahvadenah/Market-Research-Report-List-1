<p><p>The servo actuators market is primarily divided into two types: AC servo actuators and DC servo actuators. AC servo actuators utilize alternating current and are favored for their efficiency and high performance in applications requiring precise control over speed and position. DC servo actuators, powered by direct current, are known for their simplicity and are commonly employed in smaller applications that require quick response times. Both types play crucial roles in robotics, automation, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
