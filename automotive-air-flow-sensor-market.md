<p><strong>The Automotive Air Flow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane Meter Type</li><li>Hot Wire Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air flow sensor market encompasses various types, including the vane meter type and hot wire type. The vane meter type uses a mechanical vane to measure air flow, providing reliable readings but generally being bulkier. In contrast, the hot wire type employs a heated wire element that detects air flow based on cooling effects, allowing for precision measurements in a compact design. Both types are essential for optimizing engine performance and emissions control, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
