<p><p>The Lipids Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) market is categorized into three main types: Simple Lipids, Compound Lipids, and Derived Lipids. Simple Lipids, such as triglycerides and fatty acids, serve as basic energy sources and excipients in drug formulations. Compound Lipids, which include phospholipids and glycolipids, play crucial roles in drug delivery systems and membrane functions. Derived Lipids, such as fatty alcohols and sterols, are modified lipids that offer specific therapeutic properties and enhance solubility and bioavailability in pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
