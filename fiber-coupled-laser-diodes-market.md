<p><p>Fiber coupled laser diodes are essential in various applications, including telecommunications and industrial processes. The market for these diodes is primarily divided into two types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode laser diodes offer a narrow output beam with minimal dispersion, making them ideal for long-distance transmission and high-precision applications. In contrast, multimode laser diodes emit light through multiple paths, providing higher power and bandwidth over shorter distances, suitable for networking and short-range communication tasks.</p></p>
