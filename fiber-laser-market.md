<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber laser market comprises two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications like cutting and welding due to their efficiency in delivering steady power. In contrast, Pulsed fiber lasers produce short bursts of high-energy light, suitable for applications requiring precision, such as marking and engraving. Both types are widely used in industries including manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical technology.</p></p>
