<p><p>The eye testing instrument market encompasses various devices essential for diagnosing and treating ocular conditions. Slit lamps provide detailed views of the eye's structure, while biometers measure eye dimensions for surgical planning. Perimeters assess visual fields, and tonometers gauge intraocular pressure to detect glaucoma. Fundus cameras capture retinal images, autorefractors measure refractive errors, and keratometers evaluate corneal curvature. Other instruments include optical coherence tomography (OCT) and retinal scanners, collectively enhancing eye care and precision in vision assessments.</p></p>
