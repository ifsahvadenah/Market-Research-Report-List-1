<p><p>The medical rubber gloves market is primarily divided into two main types: natural rubber gloves and nitrile gloves. Natural rubber gloves, made from latex, offer excellent elasticity and sensitivity, making them suitable for tasks requiring dexterity. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some users. Nitrile gloves, on the other hand, are synthetic and hypoallergenic, providing resistance to chemicals and punctures, making them ideal for a variety of medical procedures. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and hygiene in healthcare settings.</p></p>
