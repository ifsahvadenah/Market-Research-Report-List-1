<p><p>The Ultrasound Gastroscopes market comprises two main types: Optic Ultrasound Gastroscopes and Linear Array Ultrasound Gastroscopes. Optic Ultrasound Gastroscopes utilize optical imaging technology alongside ultrasound to provide high-resolution images of the gastrointestinal tract, aiding in diagnostics. In contrast, Linear Array Ultrasound Gastroscopes employ a linear transducer to capture images in a parallel manner, offering detailed cross-sectional views. Both types enhance minimally invasive procedures, improving patient outcomes and enabling more accurate assessments of digestive system conditions.</p></p>
