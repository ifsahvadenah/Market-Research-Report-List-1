<p><strong>The Screw-in Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole</li><li>Multipole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw-in connector market is segmented into monopole and multipole types. Monopole connectors feature a single contact point, typically used for applications requiring straightforward power or signal transmission. In contrast, multipole connectors consist of multiple contacts housed within a single connector, allowing for more complex connections, including data, power, and control signals in a single unit. Each type addresses different application needs, ensuring efficient signal integrity and reliability in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
