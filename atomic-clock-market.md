<p><p>The atomic clock market includes several key types that cater to different precision needs. Cesium beam atomic clocks provide exceptional precision and are commonly used in national timekeeping standards. Hydrogen maser atomic clocks offer extremely high stability and are used in scientific research and deep-space communications.</p></p>
