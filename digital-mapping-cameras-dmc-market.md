<p><strong>The Digital Mapping Cameras (DMC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Mapping Cameras (DMC) are specialized imaging systems used in aerial surveying and remote sensing. The market is primarily segmented into Linear Array Scanners, also known as Pushbroom scanners, and Area Arrays. Linear Array Scanners capture images in a continuous sweep, providing high-resolution data over wide areas, ideal for large-scale mapping. In contrast, Area Arrays capture images in rectangular grids, allowing for detailed analysis within confined spaces. Both technologies cater to a variety of applications, including environmental monitoring and urban planning.</p></p>
