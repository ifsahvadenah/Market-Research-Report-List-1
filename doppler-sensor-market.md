<p><p>The Doppler sensor market is segmented primarily into X-band and K-band types. X-band sensors operate in the frequency range of 8 to 12 GHz, offering high-resolution measurements suitable for automotive and aerospace applications, as they provide effective object detection and speed measurement. K-band sensors, with frequencies ranging from 18 to 27 GHz, are commonly used in traffic monitoring, law enforcement, and weather radar systems, providing improved accuracy and better performance under varying environmental conditions. Both types play crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
